Our Story

AgriBoost KE was born from firsthand observations of farming struggles in Kenya. Growing up in an agricultural community, we witnessed farmers struggling with limited access to advanced tools and unaffordable livestock feeds, especially during the dry seasons.

Fertilizer prices are rising, and dishonest practices in farming have made productivity harder. We aim to bridge these gaps by providing enhanced livestock feeds and rare, high-efficiency farming machinery for hire, empowering both small-scale and large-scale farmers to thrive.

Our solution ensures higher yields, efficiency and affordability in the face of changing farming challenges.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about AgriBoost KE

AgriBoost KE offers Enhanced Livestock Feeds, Rare Farming Machinery Rental, and Farm Consultation Services to empower Kenyan farmers.

Our feeds provide premium nutrition using locally sourced alternatives, reducing costs and ensuring availability even during dry seasons, leading to healthier livestock and better yields.

You can rent hard-to-find machines like multi-crop threshers, potato harvesters, smart irrigation drones, and laser weed control machines, giving you access to high-tech tools without the high purchase cost.

Our expert consultations offer guidance on maximizing farm productivity, detecting fake fertilizers, and effective machinery use, all aimed at optimizing your yields and reducing losses.

Sustainability is one of our core values. We focus on solutions that contribute to lasting prosperity for Kenyan agriculture, from local feed sourcing to eco-friendly machinery options.
